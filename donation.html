DONATION

We are all behind you. All of the funds raised by our outstanding events are donated to the charities listed below. Please feel free to donate to these outstanding organizations in any amount. - The QueerOut Team

CENTER FOR BLACK EQUITY

More than ever, it is essential to acknowledge and accept our Black LGBTQ+ people. Recognizing this, the Center for Black Equity markets itself as the premier source for Black LGBTQ+ equality. The best thing about this agency is how deeply it engages with locals and integrates their problems with its own. Increasing awareness is the best approach to assist. It's important to elevate Black LGBTQ+ brands in addition to Black-owned businesses. And whenever you can, contribute by giving money.

SAGE

Although they might not be the ones dancing on the floats in Pride parades, LGBTQ+ senior persons are a group that is frequently disregarded. New York-based SAGE seeks to change that. SAGE represents the community's interests at the federal, state, and local levels as the biggest and oldest organization devoted to enhancing the lives of senior LGBTQ+ individuals and the caregivers who support them.

HETRICK-MARTIN INSTITUTE

The Hetrick-Martin Institute, which focuses on LGBTQ+ youth education and safety, played a role in helping to establish the Harvey Milk School. The Harvey Milk School, founded in 1985 by HMI and the New York City Department of Education, was the first institution of higher learning to cater to LGBTQ+ students.

MARSHA P JOHNSON INSTITUTE

If you're not familiar with Marsha P. Johnson's background, you should immediately visit Netflix. All across the world, the Marsha P. Johnson Institute fights for the rights of Black transgender persons. Currently, this kind of service is required. Johnson played a significant role in the Stonewall uprising in 1969 and is remembered as a significant figure in BIPOC LGBTQ+ history.
