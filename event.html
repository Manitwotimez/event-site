EVENTS

Without a doubt, the incredible activities we planned for QueerWeek rank among the most amazing concepts we have ever thought of to encourage you to express your inner pride. Don't worry, fellow queers, if you are unable to attend an event you would like to during the week of June 23–June 26. QueerOut will be there to support you.

PHOTO BASH AT CITY HALL

Show your pride by posing with your friends at QueerOut by coming to our kickoff here in City hall. We will be located in Philadelphia, Pa from 12pm-5pm June 23rd with free admission! Food & Drinks will be provided.

PHILLY QUEEROUT PARADE

Our biggest event to kick off Queer Week is the Pride Parade! To celebrate the anniversary of the Stonewall incident in the 1960’s we will continue to march to support our fellow members of the LBGTQ+ Community. The event will takes place in GayBorhood Philadelphia at 2pm for kick off and the parade will begin at 4pm June 24th! Show your pride and QUEEROUT!

PHILLY DRAG SHOW

From the creators of Rupaul’s Drag Race Queerout is proud to present a can’t miss event featuring america’s favorite drag queens! With a special appearence from her queen herself Rupaul and Megan Thee Stalion. Catch us at Level up from 10pm-1am with a $15 dollar entry on June 25th. *Must be 18+ years to attend.

GLOW IN THE DARK BASH

End the week by going to our all out Glow in the dark bash on June 26th at 8pm-1am at Woody’s located in Gayboorhood in Philadelphia! The price for this amazing event is $25 entry but if you show up during 8pm-9pm you’ll pay $15 entry. *We recommend you get here early!

COMPANY STATEMENT

To honor and encourage queer indivivuals to become apart of the amazing 3-5 day event that showcases The LBGTQ community while creating memories with fellow members and learning more about yourself and the importance of gay rights.
